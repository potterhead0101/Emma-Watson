I am Emma Watson

Miss Watson is a well-known public figure best known for her role as Hermione Granger in the Harry Potter series. Since her rise to fame, she has since used her popularity in ways that many would find admirable. Emma Watson has grown into a well-defined leader amongst the masses due to her contribution to gender equality, her leadership qualities, and generally being a role model. She has also remained down to earth and maintained a comforting and approachable demeanor that a leader should have.
